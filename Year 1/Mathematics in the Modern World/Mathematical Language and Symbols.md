The Mathematical language, similar to the English language is a way to communicate and abstract ideas into patterns of symbols and  meta-patterns of patterns.

# Nature of the Mathematical Language
1. **Precision** in mathematics make very fine distinctions among set of symbols.
2. Mathematical language **must be concise or shows simplicity**. Being concise is a strong part of the culture in mathematical language that can briefly express long sentences.
3. Mathematical language must also be **powerful**. It is a way of expressing complex thoughts with relative ease..

# English Language vs Mathematical Language
An **expression** is a meaningful combination of mathematical symbols—like numbers, variables, operations, and brackets—built according to specific rules to show a calculation or value.

A **sentence** *(or mathematical sentence*) makes.a statement about two expressions, either using numbers, variables or a combination of both. A mathematical sentence can also use symbols or words like equals, greater than, or less than.
