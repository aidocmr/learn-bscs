## Symmetry
The sense of harmonious and beautiful proportion of balance or an object is
invariant to any various transformations. *(Layman's Terms: Something that is beautifully arranged in a way that when rotated, moved, or scaled will still look close or the same to the original)*.

There are two types of symmetries, and those are the following:

| Bilateral Symmetry                                                                                                                                   | Radial Symmetry                                                                                                                                              |
| :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| A type of symmetry where cutting the object will produce two mirror images.                                                                          | A type of symmetry where certain rotations does not affect how it looks.                                                                                     |
| ![[bilateral-sym-butterfly.png]]<br>*If you take both the butterfly's wings and compare them, you'll see that they're mirror images of one another.* | ![[radial-sym-lily.png]]<br>*When you rotate a water lily, it would still look the same as it was before, because the petals revolve around a single point.* |
