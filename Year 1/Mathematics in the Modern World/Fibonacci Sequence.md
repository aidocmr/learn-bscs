The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where to get the next number, you take the previous number and add it to the current one.

$$X_{n+1} = X_{n} + X_{n-1}$$

The sequence **SHOULD ALWAYS** start with $[0, 1]$ and then is continued using the formula above.
