4. Understand the difference of relation and function.
5. Be familiar with the properties of two binary operation

# What is Mathematics?
Mathematics is the study of patterns that we see in everyday life. It's the backbone our generation and is the fundamental element to many sciences. It's also used for representing our world quantitatively and for organizing structures in a more abstract representation.
